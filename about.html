About Ralph Lawal Group

A growing multi-division company built on the belief that strategy, creativity, and genuine partnership create lasting value.

Who We Are

Built in Dublin. Connected Globally.

Ralph Lawal Group is a multi-division firm headquartered in Dublin, Ireland, with deep roots and active work in Nigeria. We bring together marketing, media, technology, education, and consulting under one roof — not as separate silos, but as connected disciplines that strengthen each other.

We were built on the conviction that quality work, genuine relationships, and clear thinking produce better outcomes than volume and noise. Every division reflects this belief.

We work with founders, creatives, educational institutions, and growing organisations who want a partner that cares as much about the work as they do.

Our Mission

To deliver premium, purpose-driven solutions across branding, media, technology, education, and consulting — helping our clients build something that endures.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted multi-division partner for ambitious brands and organisations across Ireland, Nigeria, and beyond.

Ralph Lawal — Founder
Leadership

Ralph Lawal

Ralph is an Irish-born product designer, developer, and brand strategist with roots in Nigeria. Based in Dublin, he brings together a rare mix of technical skill, creative vision, and business acumen that shapes everything Ralph Lawal Group does.

With a background spanning branding, web development, music marketing, digital education, and consulting, Ralph built RLG on the belief that the best outcomes come from working across disciplines — not being boxed into one.

He also teaches coding through CODING4KIDSHUB and volunteer programmes across Ireland and Nigeria, believing firmly that access to digital education should not be determined by geography or income.
